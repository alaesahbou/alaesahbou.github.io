Fully Loaded Web Hosting Features

Reseller hosting is a unique option for many businesses and entrepreneurs to get a leg-up in the world of hosting – they can have access to a full server and act as a web host for other companies or people. This flexibility can mean different things for different people, but with Hostvento, it always means top of the line service and all of the options you could possibly expect.

Fully whitelabled reseller hosting

100% white label hosting means you and your company can take full ownership of Hostvento’s service, and all of the special benefits that come along with it. As a reseller, it is important to get your name out there, which is why we are happy to hand over the reins to you, while still offering all of the support any of our other clients can expect.

Complete Whitelabled Support

With Hostvento, It will be more easy for you to manage your business and clients. For technical issues you dont need to contact us every time. You can give our support mail id to your clients (which is also whitelabed), for their technical issues. We will reply to your clients on behalf of you. They will never know anyting about Hostvento. Feel free to know more about this freature.

Free and Instant Site Migrations

No matter how many sites you have, We will migrate them all for free. We prvide instant migration service which no one offer in industry. Free LetsEncrypt SSL and Free Backup

Let’s Encrypt is a free, automated, and open certificate authority (CA), run for the public’s benefit. It is a service provided by the Internet Security Research Group (ISRG). We give people the digital certificates they need in order to enable HTTPS (SSL/TLS) for websites, for free, in the most user-friendly way we can. We do this because we want to create a more secure and privacy-respecting Web. The key principles behind Let’s Encrypt are Free ,Automatic ,Secure ,Transparent. Open, Cooperative
